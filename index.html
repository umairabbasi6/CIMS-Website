Dean's/Managing Director Message

Welcome to Capital Institute of Para Medical Sciences

As the Dean/Managing Director of CIMS, I am proud to lead an institution that is dedicated to excellence in medical education and healthcare training. Our institute stands at the forefront of paramedical education, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies.

At CIMS, we believe in nurturing not just skilled professionals, but compassionate healthcare providers who will make a real difference in society. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students receive the best possible education.

Tajammal Farooq Abbasi

Dean/Managing Director
