Our Programs

FSc Medical Technology

Program Overview

A comprehensive two-year program focusing on medical laboratory technology fundamentals. Students learn essential skills in clinical laboratory procedures, diagnostic techniques, and medical equipment operation.

Course Contents

  • Clinical Chemistry
  • Hematology
  • Medical Microbiology
  • Clinical Pathology
  • Blood Banking
  • Laboratory Management

Career Opportunities

  • Medical Laboratory Technician
  • Clinical Laboratory Assistant
  • Diagnostic Center Technician

Program Details

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Qualification: FSc
  • Classes: Morning/Evening
  • Admission: Annual

BS Medical Technology (4 Years)

Program Overview

A comprehensive four-year bachelor's program in Medical Technology, providing advanced knowledge and practical skills in medical laboratory sciences.

Course Contents

  • Advanced Clinical Chemistry
  • Molecular Diagnostics
  • Immunology
  • Medical Research Methodology
  • Healthcare Management
  • Biomedical Instrumentation

Career Opportunities

  • Senior Medical Technologist
  • Laboratory Manager
  • Research Assistant
  • Quality Control Specialist

Program Details

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Qualification: BS-MT
  • Classes: Morning
  • Admission: Fall/Spring
